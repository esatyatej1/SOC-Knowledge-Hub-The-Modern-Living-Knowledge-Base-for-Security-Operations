## 🚀 Why This Exists?

Most "Awesome" SOC lists are flat link collections. They lack context, structured learning, and operational practicality. The **SOC Knowledge Hub** is designed to be:

- **Role-Based**: Don't search for everything; find what *your role* needs.
- **Maturity-Aware**: Guidance for building, growing, and scaling a SOC.
- **Framework-Aligned**: Every entry maps to MITRE ATT&CK, NIST CSF, and SOC-CMM.
- **Operator-First**: Built by SOC practitioners for SOC practitioners.
