# SOC Manager & Architecture Guide

## SOC Strategy & Governance
The SOC Manager ensures that security operations align with the business goals and risk appetite.

## Key Responsibilities
1. **Staffing & Retention:** Managing analyst burnout and build career paths.
2. **Stakeholder Communication:** Reporting KPIs to the CISO/Board.
3. **Budget Management:** Justifying tool spend vs. risk reduction.
4. **Compliance Alignment:** Ensuring SOC fulfills requirements for SOC2, HIPAA, or ISO 27001.
